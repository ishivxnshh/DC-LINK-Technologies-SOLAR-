# DC-LINK Technologies - Brand Design System
**Version 1.0 (Strict B2B Alignment)**

This document outlines the mandatory design standards to ensure visual consistency between the **DC-LINK Technologies** digital presence (website) and physical assets (brochures, print materials).

---

## 1. Color Palette
Strict adherence to this palette is required. No other blues, oranges, or variations are permitted.

### Primary Brand Color (Green)
Used for: Headings, Primary Buttons, Active States, Key Icons.
*   **Green**: `#15803d` (Pantone match: *PMS 363 C*)
*   **Dark Green (Hover/Footer)**: `#14532d` (Pantone match: *PMS 357 C*)

### Accent Color (Yellow)
Used for: Highlights, Bullet Points, Call-to-Action Emphasis, Decorative Elements.
*   **Yellow**: `#eab308` (Pantone match: *PMS 116 C*)

### Neutrals (Text & Backgrounds)
Used for: Content, Body Text, Backgrounds.
*   **Black (Headings/Strong Text)**: `#0f172a` (Rich Black) or `#000000`
*   **Body Text**: `#475569` (Slate 600) for readability on white.
*   **Backgrounds**: `#ffffff` (White) or `#f9fafb` (Gray 50).
*   **Footer Text**: `#ffffff` on Dark Green backgrounds.

---

## 2. Shapes & Corner Radius
Consistency in "feel" comes from the roundness of corners. The brand uses a modern, approachable but professional soft corner style.

*   **Standard Radius (Buttons, Inputs, Badges)**: **8px**
    *   *Usage*: "Contact Us" buttons, form input fields, tags.
*   **Large Radius (Cards, Containers, Sections)**: **16px**
    *   *Usage*: Product cards, Testimonial boxes, Contact form container, Map container.
*   **Pill/Circle**: **Full Radius**
    *   *Usage*: Icon backgrounds (circular), decorative pills.

---

## 3. Borders & Lines
Line weight conveys solidity and quality using the industry standard "Solid" style.

*   **Standard Separators**: **1px Solid**
    *   *Color*: `#e5e7eb` (Light Gray)
    *   *Usage*: Card borders, section dividers.
*   **Focus/Accent Borders**: **2px Solid**
    *   *Color*: `#15803d` (Brand Green)
    *   *Usage*: Hover states on cards, active input fields.
*   **Highlights (e.g., Nav Underline)**: **3px Solid**
    *   *Color*: `#15803d` (Brand Green)
    *   *Usage*: Active menu items, bottom bars on headers.

---

## 4. Typography
**Font Family**: `Inter` (Google Fonts)
A clean, sans-serif font representing modern engineering and clarity.

*   **Headings**: **Bold (700) / ExtraBold (800)**
    *   *Color*: Brand Green (`#15803d`) or Black (`#0f172a`).
    *   *Style*: Sentence case.
*   **Subheadings**: **SemiBold (600)**
    *   *Color*: Black or Dark Slate.
    *   *Style*: Uppercase for small labels (tracking-wide).
*   **Body Text**: **Regular (400)**
    *   *Color*: Slate 600 (`#475569`).

---

## 5. Iconography
*   **Style**: Solid or Regular (Font Awesome 6 style).
*   **Color Strategy**:
    *   Primary Icons: Brand Green (`#15803d`).
    *   Accent Icons: Brand Yellow (`#eab308`).
*   **Containers**: Icons often appear inside a standard rounded square or circle container with a light background tint of the icon color (e.g., Green Icon on 10% Opacity Green background).

---

## 6. Layout & Spacing
*   **Whitespace**: Generous. Avoid B2B clutter. Sections typically have `py-24` (96px) vertical padding.
*   **Alignment**: Left-aligned text for hero/content, Centered for section headers.
*   **Visual Weight**: High contrast. Dark text on light backgrounds.

---

## Summary Checklist for Graphic Designer
1.  [ ] **Check Green**: Is it exactly `#15803d`?
2.  [ ] **Check Yellow**: Is it exactly `#eab308`?
3.  [ ] **Check Corners**: Are content boxes rounded at ~16px and buttons at ~8px?
4.  [ ] **Check Lines**: Are accent lines 3px thick?
5.  [ ] **Check Cleanliness**: Is the background white/light grey, effectively using whitespace?
